    Time and habit techniques

    What did you find interesting?

    I would like to experiement more with the Pomodoro technique. I like the way it works and it actually reminds me of scrum with the way you split tasks up and assign "Pomodoros" to them.

    What worked for you?

    One technique that I have found to be really good when distracted is to take 10 and practice my mindfullness. I was able to come back focused on the task and push through!

    Which will you experiment further with?

    I think in future I will try the follow techniques:
    - Ask youself 4 questions a day: In the morning "What are my goals?", "What are my challenges today?" and at the end of te day "Have I reached my goals today", "What have I learnt today?"
    - The Pomodoro technique
    - The Fogg method
    - Getting in the zone training
