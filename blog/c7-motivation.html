Motivation

22 January 2016

How does life look through the 'pleasure, passion and purpose' lens?

The reason I am doing this course is because althought I liked my last job, and liked the people I worked with, I wasn't doing something I was passionate about and felt fulfilled my purpose. For me there are a couple of things that make me passionate and make me feel like I have purpose and it kind of comes down to 1) People and 2) Tech :D.

Tech is something I have always loved, read about, experimented with and got. Ever since building PCs when I was 13 I have been obsessed. Every workplace I have been in I have because the tech experience or interim IT guy. Me and technology just seem to get each other. So for me, working in the tech space is a passion and something that I won't stop until I do.

People are also really important to me. I have found over the years that my motivation comes from working with others. I feel it is really important that I work with a team of people who also love what they do. I also love helping others make their lives easier and feel that working at a tech company that did that would give me a lot of purpose. We will see!
