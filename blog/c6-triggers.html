Self regualtion

18 December 2015

How did the trigger training exercises feel?

Yeah really well. The tools that we were given, some I actually have used in the past. I know that when I feel myself strongly reacting to something I breath and then think about my previous experiences in these situations where I know I have over reacted. This helps me to know that this situation is probably going to be very similar.

Did you notice any responses in yourself?

Yes, I find that my body reacts pretty easily to those kinds of thoughts. A sick to the stomach feeling often arises which is really unpleasant.

What emotion do you want to befriend?

I want to befriend anxiety, the fear that I am not doing well or that I am failing. It is one that can really come up in me and makes me feel worthless. I know from experience that this is not the case, so that helps often to reduce the effects of anxiety.
